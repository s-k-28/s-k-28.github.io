Research

the paper

Volatility skew shifts as predictors of short-term S&P 500 returns.

I am co-authoring a quantitative finance paper with Prof. Riccardo Rebonato of EDHEC Business School, former Head of Rates & FX Analytics at PIMCO. The paper examines whether shifts in the volatility skew carry predictive information for short-term S&P 500 returns.

-17.63 bps top-minus-bottom skew quintile spread, with a bootstrap confidence interval that excludes zero. The signal is small, but it is there, and it holds up.

Under review at The Journal of Fixed Income.

Co-author

Prior role
